# Mobilé Inc. Financial Data

## Revenue
- Annual revenue: ~USD 4.8 billion
- Source: Entirely from 4G LTE handset range (Wave 1)
- Profitability: Strong legacy from cartel era, now under pressure from open competition

## Cash Position
- Status: Healthy — sufficient for immediate strategic investment
- Constraint: **USD 90M minimum cash reserve floor** (MUST NOT breach)
- Tension: Simultaneous investment demands from all three waves create significant competing pressures

## Market Share
- Current: 25% in ALL three markets (USA, Europe, Asia)
- Context: All four firms start from identical position
- Dynamic: Post-DOJ, this equilibrium is breaking down rapidly

## Borrowing
- Long-term debt: Available at ~4.8% p.a.
- Short-term emergency loans: ~7.8% p.a. (~3% premium above normal)
- Emergency loan trigger: Cash runs out
- Emergency loan consequences: Financial penalty + damaging investor signal
- Key insight: Triggering emergency loans = both cost penalty AND reputational damage

## Primary Performance Metric
- **Cumulative Total Shareholder Return (TSR)**
- Includes: Share price appreciation + dividends
- Driven by: Discounted cash flow model
- Rewards: Profitable growth
- Penalises: Financial risk

## Financial Implications for Strategy
- Every investment decision must be weighed against the $90M cash floor
- Simultaneous 5G launch + AI R&D + capacity expansion could breach the floor
- Sequencing and prioritization of investments is critical
- Revenue from 4G must be defended to fund Waves 2 and 3
- Over-investment in any single wave risks starving the others
