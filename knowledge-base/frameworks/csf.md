# Critical Success Factors (CSF) — Mobilé Inc.

## Framework Purpose
CSFs identify the essential conditions Mobilé Inc. must meet to compete effectively across its three technology waves and three global markets in 2025.

**Why CSF for Mobilé Inc.:** The case presents multiple simultaneous competitive challenges. CSF analysis helps prioritize which factors are truly critical vs. merely important, directly informing resource allocation decisions.

## Critical Success Factors by Wave

### Wave 1 (4G — Defend & Extract)
| CSF | Why Critical | Mobilé's Current Position | Gap |
|-----|-------------|--------------------------|-----|
| Cost-efficient production | Post-DOJ price competition demands lowest possible unit cost | Atlanta ($28/hr) expensive; Vietnam ($6/hr) still growing | **Moderate gap** — Vietnam needs expansion |
| Asian market pricing competitiveness | 22-30% growth market where price sensitivity dominates | Vietnam plant provides logistics advantage but capacity limited | **Moderate gap** — need more capacity |
| Cash generation maximization | 4G revenue funds ALL other strategic investments | $4.8B revenue but margins compressing post-DOJ | **Growing gap** — revenue declining |
| Supply-demand matching (JIT) | Overproduction penalty (5-10%); underproduction = permanent lost sales | JIT capability established | **No gap** — capability exists |

### Wave 2 (5G — Launch Urgently)
| CSF | Why Critical | Mobilé's Current Position | Gap |
|-----|-------------|--------------------------|-----|
| Speed to market (5G handset) | First-mover premium window is closing; 38% US already own 5G | **No 5G product at all** | **Critical gap** |
| 5G technology capability (in-house or licensed) | Must have competitive 5G technology to launch | Decision not yet made (build vs license) | **Critical gap** |
| Premium brand positioning (USA/Europe) | 5G first-mover captures loyalty and pricing power | Brand exists but no 5G product to differentiate on | **Critical gap** |
| Carrier partnerships for 5G | Carrier subsidies accelerating adoption; need carrier support for launch | Existing 4G relationships but no 5G offering | **Significant gap** |

### Wave 3 (AI-Devices — Invest for 2027)
| CSF | Why Critical | Mobilé's Current Position | Gap |
|-----|-------------|--------------------------|-----|
| AI R&D investment commitment | Delay = permanently following, not leading | Not yet committed; Apple/Samsung/Qualcomm active | **Critical gap** |
| AI chip sourcing / development | On-device AI requires specialized processors | No known AI chip capability | **Critical gap** |
| AI talent acquisition | Specialized AI/ML engineering talent required | Existing R&D team, but AI-specific expertise unknown | **Probable gap** |

### Cross-Wave CSFs
| CSF | Why Critical | Mobilé's Current Position | Gap |
|-----|-------------|--------------------------|-----|
| Financial discipline ($90M floor) | Breaching floor triggers emergency borrowing + investor damage | Cash position healthy but under pressure | **Emerging gap** |
| Three-wave resource balancing | Over-investing in one wave starves others | Currently 100% 4G — no wave diversification | **Critical gap** |
| Global coordination capability | Operating coherent strategy across USA/EU/Asia with different dynamics | Management experience exists | **Moderate gap** |

## Priority Ranking
1. **Speed to 5G market** (Wave 2) — most time-sensitive, largest competitive gap
2. **AI R&D commitment** (Wave 3) — window open but closing; irreversible consequences
3. **4G cash defense** (Wave 1) — funds everything else
4. **Financial discipline** (Cross-wave) — constraint that shapes all decisions
5. **Production capacity expansion** (Cross-wave) — 2-year lead time makes this urgent

## SWOT Feed
- **Strengths:** JIT capability, global coordination experience, existing R&D foundation
- **Weaknesses:** No 5G product (most critical CSF gap), no AI R&D commitment, 4G revenue dependency
- **Opportunities:** No competitor has permanently captured 5G first-mover position yet
- **Threats:** Every month of delay increases the CSF gap in 5G and AI
